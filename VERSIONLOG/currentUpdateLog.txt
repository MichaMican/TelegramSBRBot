New commands:
	<b>/submemes [UTC Time]</b> subscribes you to the memes service
	<b>/unsubmemes</b> unsubscribes you from the memes service

Further changes:
	- /ping now only works in private conversations with the bot
	- /idea now also logs First and last name (if set), because a username is a optional variable in telegram
	- Introducing the "Memes Service". This service provides you, when subscribed to, every 24h a top meme straight from reddit.
